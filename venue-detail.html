About This Venue

Experience the epitome of luxury at the Grand Ballroom Manhattan, an exquisite event space nestled in the heart of New York City. This stunning venue combines timeless elegance with modern sophistication, creating the perfect backdrop for your most memorable occasions.

With soaring ceilings, crystal chandeliers, and floor-to-ceiling windows offering breathtaking city views, our ballroom provides an unparalleled setting for weddings, corporate galas, and exclusive celebrations. The venue can accommodate up to 300 guests for a seated dinner or 500 for a cocktail reception.
